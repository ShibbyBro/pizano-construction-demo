FAQ

Common Questions

Clearer answers for the questions roofing and storm leads usually ask before they call.

Do you offer free roof inspections?

Yes. This concept keeps Pizano's current no-obligation quote messaging but makes it much more obvious and easier to act on.

Do you handle storm damage and restoration work?

Yes. Storm restoration is one of the strongest positioning angles in the current business and should stay front and center in outreach and on the site.

Do you work on both residential and commercial properties?

Yes. Pizano already offers both residential and commercial roofing and construction services, and this demo separates those clearly so each audience knows they are in the right place.

What areas do you serve?

Oklahoma City is the core market, with additional work in surrounding metro areas like Norman and Edmond. The current site also references Tulsa-area work.

Can you help after hail or wind damage?

Yes. The current business is positioned around storm restoration, inspections, and repair planning after major weather events.

Do you only do roofing?

No. Pizano also offers contracting work, gutters, siding, painting, and related exterior services.

How fast can someone get back to me?

The current brand messaging emphasizes fast response. This demo makes that promise more visible with stronger contact calls-to-action.

What's the best way to get a quote?

The fastest path is calling (405) 664-4014 or using the contact form to request an inspection or estimate.
